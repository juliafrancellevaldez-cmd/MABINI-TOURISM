About Mabini

Mabini is a scenic town in Batangas, Philippines, famous for its rocky beaches, coral reefs, and breathtaking underwater scenery. It’s the home of Anilao — a hotspot for divers worldwide. Mabini is a haven for nature lovers, offering hiking, snorkeling, diving, and tranquil views of the sea and mountains.

Top Attractions

  • Anilao – A diver’s paradise with vibrant marine life and coral gardens.
  • Mt. Gulugod Baboy – A beginner-friendly mountain offering panoramic sea views of Batangas Bay.
  • Sombrero Island – A hat-shaped island with white sand and clear waters.
  • Eco-Resorts – Nature-aligned accommodations with peaceful vibes.
