Top Attractions

Anilao

A diver's paradise with vibrant marine life and coral gardens. Known as the birthplace of scuba diving in the Philippines, Anilao offers some of the best underwater experiences in the country.

Mt. Gulugod Baboy

A beginner-friendly mountain offering panoramic sea views of Batangas Bay. The name means "Pig's Spine" in English, referring to its series of rolling hills.

Sombrero Island

A hat-shaped island with white sand and clear waters. Perfect for swimming, snorkeling, and beach picnics. The island gets its name from its distinctive shape.

Sea Spring

Nature-aligned accommodations with peaceful vibes. These resorts offer sustainable tourism experiences while providing comfort and connection to the natural environment.
